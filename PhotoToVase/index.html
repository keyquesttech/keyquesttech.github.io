Photo to Vase

View a 3D shape from the front and you get a 2D silhouette. We turn that silhouette into a vase-mode 3D print (e.g. a can of soda becomes a tubular shape).

1. Take a picture

Capture or upload a front-on photo of the object. The outline you select will become the vase shape.

2. Select the object

Click on the object in the image to select it. Then refine: click Add and click on the image to include more, or Remove and click to deselect. The red line is the vase outline.
